# Research Brief Generator — MMA Think Tanks

## Think Tank Reference

### MATT (Marketing Attribution Think Tank)
**Focus:** Measurement, attribution, incrementality
**Key programs:**
- BaP (Brand as Performance) — brand investment → sales conversion; brand favorables activate ~6x vs non-favorables; $3M+ invested; final study 2026
- MMGF (Movable Middles Growth Framework) — targets 5x more responsive consumers; +50% ROAS vs reach-based plans
- Project ROMI — enterprise value formula connecting marketing to finance for CMO/CFO alignment
- Marketing Measurement Quality Score — maturity benchmarking framework
- State of Measurement & Attribution — longest-running measurement study (Wave 10 releasing 2026)
**Key people:** Vassilis Bakopoulos (SVP Research), Joel Rubinson

### MOSTT (Marketing Org Strategy Think Tank)
**Focus:** Marketing org design, capabilities
**Key deliverables:**
- Capabilities Map (6 values, 90 capabilities)
- MarCaps Readiness Assessment (MRA)
- MarCaps Diagnostic
**Key finding:** 1% improvement in marketing capabilities "fit" drives +2.5% YoY revenue growth
**Key people:** Michael Pettiette

### ALTT (AI Leadership Think Tank)

**Programs:** Future Labs (see lab-summary skill), RAIL, ALC Working Groups
**Scale:** 300+ leaders from 170+ companies
**Key people:** Hassan Khater (VP), Alec Foster (AI SME), Rex Briggs (Research)

### DATT (Data & CX Think Tank)
**Focus:** Data strategy, customer experience
**Programs:** Data Maturity Assessment, CX Maturity Assessment, State of Data Privacy
**Key people:** Denise Champion, Joel Rubinson

## Brief Formats

### Board Update
**Length:** 1 page
**Structure:**
- Program name + one-line status
- Key milestone since last update
- Data point or finding (if new)
- Next milestone with date
- Decision needed (if any)

### Member Newsletter Section
**Length:** 200-300 words
**Structure:**
- Headline finding
- Why it matters for the reader's business
- How to get involved or access the full research
- CTA

### Internal Team Update
**Length:** Flexible
**Structure:**
- Status (on track / at risk / completed)
- Progress since last update
- Key findings or deliverables
- Blockers or needs
- Next steps with owners and dates

### Public-Facing Summary
**Length:** 500-800 words
**Structure:**
- Context: why this research exists
- Methodology (brief)
- Top findings with data
- Implications for marketers
- About MMA (boilerplate)

## Process
1. Identify which think tank and program
2. Clarify audience and format
3. Gather latest data from SharePoint Research site or recent communications
4. Draft brief in appropriate format
5. Verify all statistics and attributions
6. Present for review
