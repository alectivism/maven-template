# Member Communications — MMA Standards

## Member Segments

### CMOs / Marketing Leaders
- Decision-makers, time-scarce, results-oriented
- Care about: peer connections, competitive advantage, evidence-based strategies
- Tone: peer-to-peer, executive brevity
- Reference: board composition (GM, Uber, Meta, Home Depot CMOs), CMO Alliance (350+ board members)

### MarTech / AdTech Companies
- Solution providers, innovation-focused
- Care about: industry trends, client relationships, thought leadership positioning
- Tone: collaborative, opportunity-focused
- Reference: Future Labs partnerships, POSSIBLE sponsorship, SMARTIES recognition

### Agency / Media Companies
- Campaign executors, trend followers
- Care about: creative effectiveness, measurement, client value
- Tone: practical, data-driven
- Reference: ACE (creative scoring), measurement frameworks, MMGF results

## Communication Types

### Welcome / Onboarding
- Warm but efficient — respect their decision to join
- Highlight top 3 immediate benefits they can access
- Include key contacts (membership team: Stephen Wellman, Amanda Hyland, Shauna Peck)
- Link to: member portal, Slack workspace, upcoming events
- Follow-up cadence: Day 1 welcome, Week 2 engagement check, Month 1 value check

### Renewal Outreach
- Lead with value delivered (events attended, research accessed, connections made)
- Include member-specific engagement data if available
- Reference upcoming programs/events they'd miss
- Soft urgency — don't pressure, demonstrate value

### Program Announcements
- Clear subject line: [Program Name] — [Key Benefit]
- One paragraph: what it is and why it matters
- Bullet list: key details (dates, cost, how to participate)
- Single CTA

### Event Invitations
- Segment by relevance (don't invite everyone to everything)
- Lead with "why this event matters for you"
- Include social proof (past attendee quotes, NPS scores)

## Value Propositions by Benefit Area

| Area | Pitch |
|------|-------|
| Research | "Access $3M+ in proprietary research that quantifies what works in marketing" |
| AI Labs | "Test AI innovations with zero risk — free labs in personalization, creative, targeting, and audience discovery" |
| Peer Network | "Join 350+ CMOs and marketing leaders who share challenges and solutions openly" |
| Events | "Attend the industry's highest-rated executive gatherings (NPS 86% CMO Summit)" |
| Training | "Upskill your team with DAM (2,100+ certified) and MAC certification" |
| Measurement | "Benchmark your measurement maturity against 800+ companies" |

## Process
1. Identify audience segment and communication type
2. Check if there's relevant engagement data to personalize
3. Draft with appropriate tone for segment
4. Include specific value propositions and CTAs
5. Present for review — **always confirm before sending**
